Eivo Apple Juice

Eivo 100% Fresh Apple Fruit Juice is a rich, sparkling fruit juice made from carefully selected fresh fruits, squeezed into the most refreshing blend of natural juice with no added sugar, no preservatives and no artificial colours or flavours. It is refreshing, tasty and exciting fruit drink.
